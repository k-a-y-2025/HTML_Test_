Chris Evans is an actor from the United States, and he's really famous for playing Captain America in the superhero movies. He was born on June 13, 1981, in Boston, Massachusetts. Before becoming Captain America, he acted in movies like "Not Another Teen Movie" and "The Perfect Score." He also played the Human Torch in the "Fantastic Four" movies. Chris made his first appearance as Captain America in the movie "Captain America: The First Avenger" in 2011. He continued to play this character in other movies like "The Avengers" and "Captain America: The Winter Soldier." Besides acting, Chris tried his hand at directing with the movie "Before We Go" in 2014. Apart from superhero movies, he's been in other good films like "Snowpiercer" and "Knives Out." Chris Evans is not just an actor; he's also known for speaking out about important issues. He cares about things like climate change and encourages people to get involved in civic activities. In 2018, Chris announced that he would stop playing Captain America after the movie "Avengers: Endgame."
